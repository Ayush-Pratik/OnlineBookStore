1984

Author: George Orwell

Genre: Dystopian Fiction

Rating: ★★★★☆

Book Description

"1984" is a dystopian novel set in a totalitarian society controlled by the Party and its leader, Big Brother. The novel explores themes of surveillance, censorship, and the loss of individuality. It follows Winston Smith, who begins to question the Party’s control over reality.
